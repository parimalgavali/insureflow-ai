# Insurance Glossary

## Policy

A contract that defines insured parties, covered risks, effective dates, limits, deductibles, and policy status.

## Coverage

A specific protection within a policy, such as collision, comprehensive, liability, or property damage coverage.

## Deductible

The amount the insured is responsible for paying before covered claim payments apply.

## Claim

A request for payment or service under a policy after a loss or incident.

## FNOL

First notice of loss. The initial report that starts the claim process.

## Loss Date

The date when the incident or damage occurred.

## Claim Severity

An assessment of how serious or costly a claim may be.

## Fraud Risk

An estimate that a claim may contain suspicious patterns requiring additional review.

## Litigation Risk

An estimate that a claim may involve legal representation, injury disputes, or other litigation indicators.

## Subrogation

The process of recovering claim costs from another party that may be responsible for the loss.

## Reserve

Money set aside as an estimate of future claim payments and expenses.

## Adjuster

The insurance professional who investigates, evaluates, and manages claims.

## SIU

Special Investigation Unit. A team that investigates claims with possible fraud or serious irregularities.
